### 4. Degree Progress (NEW)
**What You See**:
- Progress bar showing % toward graduation
- Credit units earned vs. required (e.g., 60/120)

- All courses organized by level (100, 200, 300, 400)
- List of failed courses (red badges)

  - ✓ Minimum 120 credit units
  - ✓ CGPA minimum 2.0

**Why It's Useful**:
- Know exactly how close you are to graduation
- Plan remaining semesters
- Identify which courses to retake
- Ensure you meet all degree requirements
- Estimated graduation timeline

**Access**: Dashboard → "Degree Progress" Button

## 📊 Understanding Your Data

### GPA Classification
| GPA Range | Classification | What It Means |
|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| 4.0 - 3.5 | 1st Class | Excellent performance |
| 3.49 - 2.5 | 2nd Class Upper | Good performance |
| 2.49 - 2.0 | 2nd Class Lower | Satisfactory |
| 1.99 - 1.0 | 3rd Class | Below average |
| Below 1.0 | Probation | At risk of suspension |

### Credit Units
- Each course has a certain number of credit units (usually 1-4)
- You must complete all required courses
- Total credit units needed for degree (usually 120)

### Grade Interpretations
| Grade | Points | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| A | 4.0 | Excellent |
| B | 3.0 | Good |
| C | 2.0 | Satisfactory |
| D | 1.0 | Passing (barely) |
| F | 0.0 | Fail (must retake) |

### Academic Standing
| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Good Standing | CGPA ≥ 2.0 | Continue normally |
| Probation | CGPA < 2.0 | Improve grades next semester |
| At Risk | Very low CGPA | Urgent intervention needed |

## ⚠️ Common Alerts & What to Do

### Alert: "Failed Courses"
**Meaning**: You have F grades that need to be retaken
**Action**: 
- Click "View Details" in alert
- Go to degree_progress page to see which courses
- Register for them in the next semester

### Alert: "Academic Probation"  
**Meaning**: Your CGPA fell below 2.0
**Action**:
- Click "View Status"
- Meet with academic advisor
- Focus on improving grades
- Consider lighter course load next semester

### Alert: "Excellent Performance"
**Meaning**: Your CGPA is 3.5 or above
**Action**:
- Maintain the same study habits
- Continue to perform well
- You may be eligible for honors

### Alert: "Course Registration"
**Meaning**: You haven't registered courses for this semester
**Action**:
- Click "Register Now"
- Choose your courses
- Ensure you have minimum required credits
- Complete registration before deadline

### Alert: "Pending Results"
**Meaning**: Grades haven't been published yet
**Action**:
- Be patient for lecturers to finalize grades
- Check back in a few days
- Contact lecturer if grades are very late
